# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-12

`GPTlagfixer` is designed to work fully locally in your browser.

## What this extension does

The extension helps long ChatGPT conversations stay responsive by limiting how many recent messages are rendered at once.

## Data collection

This extension does not collect, sell, or transmit your personal data to the developer or to any third party.

This extension does not use:

- analytics
- tracking
- advertising identifiers
- remote logging
- a backend server

## Data stored locally

The extension stores a small amount of local data in your browser to make its features work correctly.

This may include:

- whether the extension is enabled
- how many messages remain visible
- how many older messages load each time
- temporary per-chat state needed to manage hidden or revealed message ranges

This data stays on your device and is not sent to the developer.

## Website access

The extension runs only on:

- `https://chatgpt.com/*`
- `https://chat.openai.com/*`

It needs access to these pages so it can reduce how many messages are rendered in long conversations.

## Third-party services

The extension itself does not connect to any third-party service for its core functionality.

If you choose to click the optional support link, you may be taken to Ko-fi. That visit is voluntary and is governed by Ko-fi's own policies.

## Changes to this policy

If the extension's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated before or at the time those changes are released.
